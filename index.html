Alejandro Garnacho - Manchester United’s Rising Star

Explore His Journey

Facts

Alejandro Garnacho, born July 1, 2004, in Madrid, is a dual Argentine-Spanish left winger for Manchester United. Joined from Atlético Madrid in 2020 for £420,000, debuted in 2022 vs. Chelsea, and scored his first goal vs. Real Sociedad. He’s won the 2022 FA Youth Cup, 2024 FA Cup (scoring vs. Manchester City), 2023 Puskás Award for an overhead kick vs. Everton, and 2024 Copa América with Argentina.

Playing Style

A dynamic left winger with explosive pace, skillful dribbling, and flair. He cuts inside with his right foot for powerful shots or precise crosses. His agility, directness, and spectacular moves—like his award-winning overhead kick—draw comparisons to a young Cristiano Ronaldo.
